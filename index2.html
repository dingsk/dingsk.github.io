About MetaEfi

MetaEfi aims to play a critical role in the mass adoption of Web 3 by building a bridge to connect the real world and Web 3.

We turn the land - one of the scarcest resources in our planet into the bond that connects traditional industries and Web 3 communities, foster trust between two sides. We could therefore utilize the online and offline identities to financially and non-financially benefit members in our ecosystem, allowing them to better connect, grow, prosper and thrive in the new era.

Roadmap

  • October 2022

    Project Goldrush

    Purchase a land in NSW, Australia and proceed as planned, profits will be distributed accordingly depending on whether mine is discovered or not. This world’s biggest blind box not just simply offer you an opportunity to discover valuable mines, but a chance to start a journey the brings real assets into Web 3.

  • February 2023

    Land-as-a-Service

    A marketplace for retail investors get access to high-quality and selective real assets NFT projects, which will be a perfect place for both Web 2 and Web 3 organisations to build up understanding and trust.

  • April 2023

    Community identity establishment

    MetaEfi community members will receive DIDs based on their preferences and characteristics, and SBTs issued by partners from both Web 2 and 3. This not only forms individual initial appearances in the digital world, but increase the synergy between Web 2 and Web 3.

  • June 2023

    Live-to-Earn

    MetaEfi will continuously empower member’s DIDs and SBTs, so that they will automatically receive daily benefits including financial and non-financial. By doing so, we give back the data ownership to individual, foster the transition from centralization to decentralization.

  • Staring of 2024

    Live-to-Connect

    Participants will be able to quickly establish social connections in the metaverse because of the DIDs they carry, together with the real world social networks, a true seamless integration between the real and the digital begins.
